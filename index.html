About BGMI

BGMI, or Battlegrounds Mobile India, is a popular battle royale game developed and published by Krafton for the Indian market. It was launched after PUBG Mobile was banned in India, offering a similar experience tailored for Indian players.

History of BGMI

Initial Launch as PUBG Mobile

Originally known as PUBG Mobile, the game was first introduced globally in 2018. It quickly became popular due to its immersive battle royale experience.

Ban and Rebranding as BGMI

In 2020, PUBG Mobile was banned in India due to security concerns. To re-enter the Indian market, Krafton launched Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) in 2021, rebranded with compliance to local regulations.

Current Popularity

Since its re-release, BGMI has maintained a strong player base in India, consistently updating the game with new features, events, and maps tailored for Indian users.
